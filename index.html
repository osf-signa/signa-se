# What We Do

We are very good at automating manual processes. Especially processes that involve many people over a long time. Such processes tie up a lot of human resources. Often these processes can be replaced with clever use of Information Technology. That is precisely what we do. We place burden where it belongs. On the back of a machine.

Our mission is clear: we shift the burden from humans to machines, freeing your team to focus on high-value tasks that truly require human insight and creativity.

# About Us

Founded in 2007, Signa has been at the forefront of systems development and IT consulting. Our core team, based in Stockholm, Sweden, leverages a vast network of exceptional developers and testers to deliver cutting-edge solutions.

We're experts in business analysis and project management, with plenty of real-world experience under our belts. Our small but mighty team means we can move fast, adapt quickly, and give each client's project the attention it deserves. We're not just working for you – we're invested in your success.
